A recipe for banana bread incase you want to make some yourself.

Ingredients Amount
Flour 440g
Backing Powder 4 teaspoons
Natron 1/2 teaspoon
salt 1 teaspoon
Butter 150g
Sugar 300g
Eggs 4
Mashed Bananas must be very ripe 2
Cinnamon just a little

Insert into bowl mix A LOT then bake for 30-40 minutes at 180°C
